How it works

Make a request
Request for a free site inspection by filling in our contact form and we will contact you within 24 hours to arrange for a site inspection at your office.
We evaluate your needs
At Nimbus, we know every workspace is unique and have different labour and management requirements. This is why we send our workspace consultants down to visit your site, understand what makes your space tick and then provide you with a tailored solution that meets your unique requirements.
We provide a tailored solution
Based on your requirements, we will find you a cleaner best suited to your space and also plan out a detailed daily task schedule for our workers to follow. The technology that we’ve built ensures anyone from Nimbus who enters your premises knows the nitty gritty details of your space so you never have to repeat your instructions again.
We support you full time
We believe in giving a personal touch. Every client of Nimbus has a dedicated account manager available all days of the week for immediate support. We also have a large team of standby workers to ensure your workspace runs smoothly. On top of that, our office management platform automates many all your office management headaches and allows you to be in three places all at once. So sit back, relax and let us manage your workspace.
