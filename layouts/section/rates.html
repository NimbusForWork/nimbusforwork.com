Q: What is a part-time maid?

A part-time maid in Singapore does part-time housework with several employers. No Foreign Domestic Worker with a valid work permit is allowed to do part-time.

A part-time maid is either a Singaporean or a permanent resident or a foreigner who has a “Visit Pass” (looks like an Employment Pass green card but is written on it “Visit Pass”). Before hiring a part-time maid, always check her ID and work pass if any. It is an offence to employ as a part-time helper a Foreign Domestic Worker who has a valid work permit with another employer.

All of Trust Cleanz maids are insured. An introduction and an agreement is signed upon engagement of our part-time maids.
